Brain Power & Cortisol Regulation — 6-Week Transformational Course

This six-week course helps participants understand and regulate the stress-cortisol cycle to improve mental clarity, emotional balance, and nervous system resilience through practical daily tools.

Expected outcomes

  • Understand how cortisol patterns affect cognition, mood, recovery, and daily performance.
  • Identify personal stress triggers and autonomic dysregulation patterns.
  • Apply breathwork and vagal tone techniques to downshift stress states quickly.
  • Build a personalized lifestyle plan for long-term cortisol balance and brain support.

Weekly structure (draft)

  • Week 1: Stress physiology, cortisol basics, and brain-function foundations.
  • Week 2: Vagus nerve education and nervous system reset practices.
  • Week 3: Nutrition and supplementation strategies for cortisol balance.
  • Week 4: Breathwork and emotional regulation implementation.
  • Week 5: Sleep optimization and recovery protocols.
  • Week 6: Neuroplasticity, habit lock-in, and long-term resilience planning.

Who this is for

  • Adults experiencing chronic stress, cognitive fatigue, or inconsistent energy.
  • Participants who want practical nervous system tools they can use daily.
  • People ready to apply repeatable sleep, breath, nutrition, and regulation habits.

Included materials (planned)

  • Stress tracking worksheet and cortisol symptom checklist.
  • Breathwork guide and nervous system reset guide.
  • Sleep optimization checklist and recovery planning prompts.
  • Food guidance list and lifestyle integration templates.
Medical safety note: This course is educational and informational only and is not intended to diagnose, treat, or cure medical conditions. Consult qualified healthcare professionals before beginning new supplements, dietary changes, or wellness practices.
