Brain Power & Cortisol Regulation Workbook

Feeling wired but tired, foggy by mid-afternoon, and somehow exhausted and buzzing at the same time? You are not broken, your cortisol is just running the show. Brain Power & Cortisol Regulation is a warm, practical six-week guided workbook that helps you understand the stress hormone behind brain fog, mood swings, and 3 a.m. wake-ups, then teaches practical ways to restore calm.

Inside you'll discover:
* How cortisol works, and why wired-but-tired is a stress signal, not a personal failure
* Daily practices, breathwork, and mind-body techniques that calm your nervous system
* Week-by-week lessons, prompts, and trackers for real-world progress
* Practical ways to sharpen focus, steady mood, and reclaim mental clarity
