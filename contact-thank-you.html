WELCOME, FROM DR. JESSIE

Hi, I'm Dr. Jessie Keener.

Naturopathic Physician, breath worker, Human Intelligence Specialist, occasional channel for the Two Marys, and, full disclosure, someone who's been doing this work since 1987 from a small corner of Central Texas.

I came to this path the hard way: I lost my own health in my early twenties (full-blown menopause at 26, fainting spells, pain syndromes, the works), found my way back through real natural medicine and a brilliant mentor named Dr. Theresa Dale, and then spent the next three-plus decades figuring out what actually works for the body, brain, breath, and the spirit running the whole show.

Thousands of patients, hundreds of interviews, a TV health show, a book, and a lot of laughing later, here we are.

You're now in a small, surprisingly wonderful corner of the internet where we take healing seriously, ourselves a little less so, and the connection between mind and body very, very seriously.

Welcome.
